About Us

Shreeram Automobiles was established in the year 1985 with Excellent manufacturing facilities at 2nd Phase Industrial Area at Adityapur, Jamshedpur. Its main focus is to manufacture high quality Tipper Body, sheet metal component & heavy fabrication jobs. Under the leadership & Guidance of Mr. G.P. Sharma (Proprietor), the company has 35 years of rich experience in the manufacturing of tipper bodies, buses and containers. During the initial day of Mr. G.P. Sharma , he was associated with TML. authorized body building companies like Utkal Automobiles & General Sales, Sun Orange Ltd towards manufacturing of tipper bodies. Shreeram Automobiles has full-fledged highly equipped workshop for Process development, Product development, heavy and medium fabrication, structural fabrication. Sheet Metal Component, Tipper Body building, construction equipment & mass production with quality and on time delivery. We have also a expert team of highly skilled workmen, experienced supervisor & engineers to achieve a target for the company as well as capable to meet customer's requirement. Our strong focus lies in environment, safety and welfare activities for employees and society
